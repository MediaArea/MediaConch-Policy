MD5

MD5 message-digest algorithm.

Used in Qt Core (QCryptographicHash). Configure with -DQT_CRYPTOGRAPHICHASH_ONLY_SHA1 to avoid.

The sources can be found in qtbase/src/3rdparty/md5.
