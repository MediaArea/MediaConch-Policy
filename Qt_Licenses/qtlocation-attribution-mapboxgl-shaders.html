Mapbox GL Shaders

Shaders used by Mapbox GL

Used in the Mapbox GL plugin of Qt Location.

The sources can be found in qtlocation/src/3rdparty/mapbox-gl-native/src/3rd_party/mapbox-gl-shaders.
